Creating an Ethereum Wallet


Creating an ETH Wallet is fast and easy. Just open either Trust Wallet OR Coinbase Wallet and create a wallet.
Just follow the instructions and SAVE YOUR 12-KEY SEED PHRASE.

Adding Funds


In order to join Summit-ETH, you need to have Ethereum.
One of the easiest way to have Ethereum in the Philippines is to use Coins.PH wallet.



1. Download and Install Coins.PH from Google Play Store (Android) or App Store (iOS) and register.

2. You need to have a Verified Coins.PH Account in order to proceed.
2.1 - You can ask your friend to cash-in for you instead while you wait (max of 3 days verification).


3. Once you are a Verified Coins.PH user, cash-in a minimum amount of 900 pesos (as of July 01, 2020).
3.1 900 pesos breakdown:
      30 pesos - coins.ph cash-in fee
      80 pesos - coins.ph ETH wallet unlock
      790 pesos to eth ~ 0.068 eth
      0.008 eth coins.ph to trust-wallet/coinbase-wallet transfer fee
      0.02 eth blockchain transaction fee upon registration
      0.04 eth registration fee on summit-eth
*** the price of Ethereum fluctuates so prices are estimations only!
3.2 Coins.PH has many cash-in options, for this tutorial we use Palawan Pawnshop with a cash-in fee of 30 pesos.


4. After cashing-in, unlock your ETH wallet (80 pesos).


5. Convert remaining cash from PHP to ETH.


6. Open TrustWallet/CoinbaseWallet and copy your Wallet Address.
Trust Wallet

Coinbase Wallet


7. Switch back to Coins.PH and select [SEND] from [eth] tab (slide left).
7.1 Tap Enter ETH Wallet Address.


8. Hold on the textbox and paste your Wallet Address.


9. Calculate your total ETH - ETH fee. Double check the amounts!


10. Send and wait for the transaction to be recorded in the blockchain. You can wait in your Wallet App.

11. Congratulations, you now have Ethereum in your wallet!

Using Metamask (Android App)


1. First open metamask app and tap on the menu icon on the upper left corner.
2. Tap on Browser.
3. Tap the plus icon (+) to create a new tab.
4. Tap on the url textbox and paste/type in https://summit-eth.com/
** If you have been invited, ask for your referrer's invite link.
5. You can now connect your wallet to summit-eth!

Using Metamask (Chrome Extension)


1. Go to https://metamask.io/download.html and download the metamask extension.
2. Install the extension and follow the on-screen instructions.
3. Here you can restore or create a new Ethereum wallet.
4. Go to https://summit-eth.com/ and connect your wallet!
