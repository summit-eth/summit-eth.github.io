Adding Funds


In order to convert your fiat currency into Ethereum, you need to follow how you can add funds to your wallet on each app. Just follow the instructions given on each app.


Additionally, here are their respective links:

Trust Wallet - minimum of $50 cash-in from card - https://trustwallet.com/buy-ethereum

Coinbase Wallet - transfer from Coinbase - https://help.coinbase.com/en/coinbase/getting-started#adding-a-payment-method

Switching between wallet Apps


YOU MUST KEEP YOUR 12 PHRASE SEED WORDS SOMEWHERE SAFE!
Switching between wallet apps is fast. You only need your 12-phrase seed words generated when you create a wallet.
The following is an example on how I reopened my wallet on coinbase.

Next I entered my 12-phrase seed words and set up my account.

Using Trust Wallet


1. First open trust wallet and tap on the four boxes(app) icon below.
2. Tap on the textbox and paste/type in https://summit-eth.com/
** If you have been invited, ask for your referrer's invite link.
3. You can now connect your wallet to summit-eth!

Using Coinbase Wallet


1. First open coinbase wallet app and tap on the four boxes(app) icon below.
2. Tap on the textbox and paste/type in https://summit-eth.com/
** If you have been invited, ask for your referrer's invite link.
3. You can now connect your wallet to summit-eth!

Using Metamask (Android App)


1. First open metamask app and tap on the menu icon on the upper left corner.
2. Tap on Browser.
3. Tap the plus icon (+) to create a new tab.
4. Tap on the url textbox and paste/type in https://summit-eth.com/
** If you have been invited, ask for your referrer's invite link.
5. You can now connect your wallet to summit-eth!

Using Metamask (Chrome Extension)


1. Go to https://metamask.io/download.html and download the metamask extension.
2. Install the extension and follow the on-screen instructions.
3. Here you can restore or create a new Ethereum wallet.
4. Go to https://summit-eth.com/ and connect your wallet!
